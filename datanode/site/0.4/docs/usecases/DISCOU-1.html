DISCOU-1.js

Excerpt

Have a dataset of items and related topics, annotated with a relevance score. Given an item, find the 10 items with the most similar topics, listed in order of relevance and displayed with type, title, summary, location and the most relevant common topic.

Synopsys

  1. having a dataset of resources and topics >> Dataset, Resource, Topic
  2. having a single resource from that dataset >> Single, receive
  3. identify items having topics in common with the resource >> identify, SharedFeature
  4. rank them according to a score representing how much important are the common topics for both resource >> rank, Criteria, Score, Representation, Relevance, SharedFeature, ResourcePair
  5. truncate 10 exemplars according to rank >> truncate, Number, 10, Exemplar, Criteria, Rank
  6. describe the exemplars with title, summary >> describe, Exemplar, Summary, Title
  7. explain by the most important shared common topic >> explain, Criteria, Best, SharedFeature
