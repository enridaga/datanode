Excerpt

From the 188 properties linking two artists, we identified that 18 were used as links between artists while it was not their main purpose8, such as the property dbprop:notableInstruments — used to link an artist to its instrument(s) — or dbprop:nationalAnthem — linking a country to its anthem.
